Some information about me

Introduction

My name is Garrison Parrish. I am a student at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, class of 2023. I'm currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science.

Mission

I am passionate about computer science. As a Learning Assistant for COMP 110, UNC's introductory computer science course, I play a part in introducing many of my fellow students to the joy of programming. My favorite thing about my work is that I have the opportunity to help

There are few things more inspiring than the thrill of learning something new and useful. I came into UNC as an economics major, and while I learned a great deal I didn't enjoy it. After the COVID-19 pandemic hit the US in spring 2020, I started to reconsider my major. I wanted to pursue something that married my passion for creativity and my love of problem-solving, and so I decided to take COMP 110. That decision was one of the best in my life, because programming sparked my curiosity and passion like no other class had and I switched my major. I love computer science because it is both deeply technical and incredibly creative--there are often several ways of solving a problem, but the optimal solution requires a logical and elegant way of thinking. As an LA I want to share that passion with students and I always approach a question as an opportunity to teach something new.
